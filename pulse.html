Pulse


What is Pulse?

Pulse is a form of social media that is similar yet different to others such as Reddit and Twitter.
You share your lives and things you like similar to other social media platforms.
However, on pulse you can decide whether this will be anonymous or through a registered account.
Pulse is a more "decentralized" form of social media without ads, and without corporate interference.
The goal here is for people to share their lives online and to see what their friends are doing as well.

Will Pulse change overtime or will it always look the way it does?

Pulse is Brand new. it is in its earliest phase currently. It will, over time, look much better and work much faster.
Pulse will become easier and more fun to use.



Pulse lets you define how you want to share the occurrences of your daily life.
People do not have to know who did a certain thing since that user can choose to be in anonymous mode.
The main goal here is to share what is going on. Whether or not you
choose to reveal it is you making these posts, is completely up to you.

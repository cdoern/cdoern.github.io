What is Serenity?

Serenity is a productivity management app. Its main goal is to help you focus! The extension allows you to input sites that you don't want to visit while working, and a set duration you'd like to work.

How does this work?

After all of the information is plugged in, you can click on Serenity's icon in the top right of your chrome window, revealing a popup! This popup contains a cityscape with a lone worker in his apartment. His goal is the same as yours, get his work done in the time allotted. This worker needs to work all throughout the night in order to finish by rush hour! He has a huge presentation in the morning. The extension tracks which sites you go to while working. Going to one of your blacklisted sites will result in an decrease in the amount of hours until rush hour for the lone worker. If you visit too many of your blacklisted sites during your working time, it can result in time running out before he finishes his presentation.

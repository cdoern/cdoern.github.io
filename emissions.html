Emissions Bot

What is this bot?

This is a comprehensive emissions data bot. it takes in your personal usage and calculates by following this link you can take the survey which will display you average emissions per year from home heating/power as well as vehicle usage and personal waste.

How can I use the bot outside of Reddit?

You can email/text emissionsbot@gmail.com to get a survey. This feature is in BETA but should work with any service provider.
